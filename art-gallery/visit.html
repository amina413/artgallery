Welcome to Our Art Gallery

Discover creativity, connection, and culture at our vibrant space!

Visit Us

Best Time to Visit:We recommend visiting during the weekdays between 10 AM and 2 PM for a quieter experience. Weekends are ideal for families and group activities.

What to Do: Explore our gallery exhibits, join interactive art sessions, or relax at our serene café with a selection of artisanal treats.

We also offer guided tours to help you dive deeper into the stories behind our collections. Don’t forget to check out the gift shop for unique keepsakes!

Painting Classes for Kids

Unleash your child’s inner artist in our fun and interactive painting classes! Designed for kids aged 6-12, these sessions blend creativity with hands-on learning in a supportive environment.

Highlights:

Guided by experienced art instructors

All materials provided

Take-home masterpieces

Schedule: Every Saturday at 11 AM and 2 PM.

Duration: 90 minutes

Spaces are limited! Reserve your spot today and let your child’s imagination soar.

Family Workshops

Looking for a meaningful way to spend time with your loved ones? Our family workshops bring people together through art! These sessions are perfect for parents, grandparents, and kids to bond while creating something beautiful.

What to Expect:

  • Collaborative art projects
  • Fun challenges and activities
  • Memories that last a lifetime

Schedule: Sundays at 1 PM

Duration: 2 hours

Don’t miss this opportunity to reconnect and make lasting memories. Sign up now!
